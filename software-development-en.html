Software Development

DAISYBYTES can offer its software development service either directly on-site in Japan or remotely around the world. That includes continuous iterations of:

Analysing Business Needs

  • by fully grasping the actual situation
  • by identifying the right problems to solve
  • by choosing the best open-source tools to do the job
  • by reacting quickly to new client demands

Modeling Data

  • for the overall system structure
  • for effective, non-redundant usage in common databases (e.g. Datomic, Postgresql, MySQL)
  • for easy modifications in the future

Building Infrastructure

  • for inhouse or cloud-based deployment
  • for solid security
  • for thorough encryption-based data protection
  • for scalable setups with maximum availability
  • for easy and cost-effective maintenance
  • running on powerful and reliably-secure Linux/BSD systems

Setting up Continuous Integration

  • to increase development speed
  • to increase the quality of your product through automated tests
  • to decrease developers' workload by eliminating overhead
  • either in the cloud or on your premises
  • using open-source technology (e.g. CircleCI, Jenkins, Travis)

Implementing Business Logic

  • in simple, consistent, easy-to-extend, Clojure (Java) based interfaces
  • also in form of RESTful (micro)services
  • that works well with other components within the big-picture

Creating User Interfaces

  • that are responsive, uncluttered and easy to understand
  • that have optimized UX by quickly and continuously applying user feedback
  • that are easy to use for anyone
  • that can easily be brushed-up by external artists or graphic designers
  • that make use of ClojureScript, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery and various other web frameworks

Training Your Users

  • by providing easy-to-understand and complete documentation
  • by providing personal introductions for your staff
Please have a closer look at the applied technology and how some of this could be done within your project.
