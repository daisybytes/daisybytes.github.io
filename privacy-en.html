Privacy Policy

General

DAISYBYTES strives to protect your privacy as well and far as possible. On this page you can inform yourself about the personal information gathered and used when visiting this website. If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact DAISYBYTES.

Cookies

Tracking & Analytics

Correspondance

When contacting DAISYBYTES, any information you provide will be stored for the purpose of processing your request. Your personal information will never be passed to third parties.

Note: DAISYBYTES uses a commercial email service provider (ESP) based in Germany that is known for its excellent privacy support. Even if you use SSL/TLS to connect to your own ESP, emails will be transmitted unencrypted between a numerous count of third party servers of different ESPs afterwards. Any of those ESP thus has unrestricted access to any unencrypted contents sent to and from DAISYBYTES. This basically means that all unencrypted email messages must be considered as being also read by third parties. In order to protect your privacy, please encrypt your emails when sending sensitive information to DAISYBYTES. DAISYBYTES cannot be held responsible for any potential leaks of information etc. received via unencrypted emails.

Data Security

Your data is important to DAISYBYTES, but please remember that unfortunately there is no 100% secure method of transmitting data via the Internet, nor a 100% secure method to electronically store data. Though DAISYBYTES will always strive to protect your personal information, it cannot take responsibility for the absolute security of that information.

Server Protocols

When you visit this website, the following data is collected and stored for 14 days in a server-side log file. This is done for technical reasons in order to run and especially to protect this website against possible attacks:

  • IP address
  • operating system
  • browser type and version
  • page(s) visited (URL)
  • in case you followed a link, the page you came from (referrer URL)
